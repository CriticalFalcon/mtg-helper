MTG Helper

Find cards, build decks, and compare lines of play all in one place.

Quick, interactive deck analysis tailored to Magic: The Gathering players.

Core features

Card Search

Search by name, set, type, mana cost, color, rarity, and text. Access print details and quick card insights.

Deck Compare

Compare two decks instantly. See card overlap, mana curves, color spread, and matchup balance.

How to start

  1. Go to Card Search and find the cards you want.
  2. Use Deck Compare to load two decks or create new ones.
  3. Analyze the results and tune your deck for consistency and strength.
  4. Use side-by-side metrics to decide your final mainboard cards.

Player tips

  • Begin with a clear strategy: Aggro, Control, Combo, or Commander.
  • Filter to your preferred format and keep your deck lean.
  • Use compare stats to spot mana gaps and redundancies.
  • Test iteratively and update based on match results.
