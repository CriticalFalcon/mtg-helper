Welcome to MTG Helper

Your ultimate tool for Magic: The Gathering card management and deck building.

Updated on March 23, 2026

Features

  • Find cards by name, set, type, or price.
  • Compare decks side-by-side and analyze synergies.
  • Track favorites and build your collection database.
  • Supports advanced filtering for colors, formats, and rarity.

Getting Started

  1. Go to Card Search to look up cards.
  2. Add cards to your deck and fine-tune with the Deck Compare tool.
  3. Use the Navbar links to navigate quickly between sections.
  4. Deploy new updates with ng deploy when ready.

Tips

For best performance, use modern browsers and keep your dataset filtered to your preferred formats. Your deck can evolve quickly with the built-in suggestions where available.
